YOUR RIGHTS:

Have the Option to Watch Only Free to Air Channels against payment to network Capacity Fee.

Know the Pay Channel Tariff per Channel as well as for the Bouquets.

Select Channels and Bouquet of your own choice.

Reject or replace Channels from the Basic Bouquet of 100 Channels. (Replacing with Pay Channels would attract Fees for the selected Channels over and above Network Capacity Fee).

Buy or Rent Set Top Box.

Receive Free Service for Set Top Box within Warranty period of One Year for Owned Set top Boxes and 3 years for Rented Set Top Boxes.

Avail Services on Pre-paid or Post-paid Basis. {Your Network may ask for Refundable Deposit in case you opt for Post-paid Mode}.

Receive Invoices in Electronic or Hard Copy mode as per your choice.

Receive Spot Receipt for Payments made.

Access the Support Services Team via Toll Free Number.

Get the Request/complaint addressed within 72 Hours.

Escalate unresolved issues to Nodal Officer.

Complain to specific Independent Bodies in case of Objectionable Content or Advertisements.

Lodge complaint with Ministry of Information and Broadcasting against the Service Provider.


FEES:

Network capacity Fee of ₹.130/- for the Basic Offering of 100 Channels.

Additional FTA Channels would attract pro-rate Network Capacity fee e.g. ₹.20/- for 25 Channels.

Pay Channel charges as per MRP or NRP (Network Retail Price).

Pre-paid charges being payable on a 30 day cycle basis starting from the date of Service commencement.

Post-paid being billable on Calendar Month basis.

Set Top Box activation Fee of ₹.350/-.

Reactivation Fee of ₹.100/- for deactivated Set Top Boxes.

Pro-rata Refund/Discount for Channels that remain Off-air for 72 hours or beyond during a month.
